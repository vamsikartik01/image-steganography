What is steganography?

Steganography is the practice of hiding a secret message inside of something that is not secret. That something can be just about anything you want. Here that something is an image of your choice. In the Encode webpage upload your image and enter the messege then we will integrate the messege into the image and the new image will be downloaded. Now share the image to who ever you want but remember the image should not be compressed. To decrypt the messege simply upload the encrypted image in decode webpage.
